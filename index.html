Hi, I'm Chad Whitaker! I'm a student at Momentum Learning in Durham, NC. Some of my interests outside of Software Development include: Soccer, Disc Golf, NC State Athletics, and my dog.

I graduated from North Carolina State University in 2015 with a B.S. degree in Business Administration - Finance Concentration and a Minor in Economics. While in school, I was the treasurer and president of my fraternity, Pi Kappa Alpha.

My early work experience includes internships for the Corporate Social Responsibility department and Government Affairs department. After graduation, I joined Quintiles (now IQVIA) as a Contracts Analyst Intern. I was hired as a Contracts Analyst I the following Fall. I spent another year in that position before transferring into a Pharmaceutical Sales Representative role. During my time in this role I visited target doctors, physicians assistants, and nurse practitioners. I learned a great deal in the year and a half in this position as it was my first and only experience in Sales. At the end of May 2018, I decided to quit my job and attend Momentum to get the skills I need to be a Software Developer. Keep checking this site as I develop my portfolio!
